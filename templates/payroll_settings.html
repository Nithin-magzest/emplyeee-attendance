Statutory Deduction Rates
These rates apply to all employees. PF is on Basic salary (capped at the basic cap). LOP = Gross ÷ 26 × absent days (standard Indian payroll). TDS is annual % ÷ 12 per month.
Provident Fund (PF)
Standard: 12% of Basic — deducted from salary
Standard: 12% of Basic — company's cost, shown in slip for reference
PF computed on min(Basic, cap). At Rs.15,000 cap → max PF = Rs.1,800
Tax Deductions
Fixed flat amount per employee per month (varies by state)
Applied as: Annual CTC × rate% ÷ 12 per month. Set 0 to disable.
Employee Monthly CTC
Set Monthly CTC per employee. Daily rate auto-computed as CTC ÷ 26. Basic % splits the CTC into Basic, HRA, Conveyance, Special Allowance.
